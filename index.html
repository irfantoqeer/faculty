About Our Research Department

The Department of Physics at Riphah International University, Faisalabad Campus, is dedicated to advancing knowledge through cutting-edge research in various fields of physics. Our postgraduate program brings together talented researchers and experienced supervisors to explore fundamental questions and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Our research groups focus on interdisciplinary areas including condensed matter physics, quantum mechanics, material science, and computational physics. We foster a collaborative environment where MPhil and PhD candidates work alongside distinguished faculty members to push the boundaries of scientific discovery.
